A Legacy Rooted in Scientific Rigor

Founded at the intersection of material science and environmental activism, BioPack Industrial began as a boutique laboratory dedicated to solving the single-use plastic crisis without compromising industrial performance.

Our journey from a pilot facility to a global leader in architectural ecology is driven by a singular mission: to make biodegradable materials the standard for global commerce. We don't just manufacture bags; we engineer environmental restoration.
