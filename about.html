Our Mission

To bridge the gap between industrial necessity and environmental responsibility. We leverage advanced PBAT (Polybutylene adipate terephthalate) and biofiller technologies to provide high-performance, sustainable alternatives to traditional plastics without compromising on strength or scalability.

Our Vision

We envision a world where the word "disposable" is no longer synonymous with "permanent." ABI Bio Bags Udyog aims to lead the global shift toward a circular economy by providing the reliable supply chain backbone required for large-scale ecological transition.

The Foundation

Founded as a dedicated unit for bio-polymer research, ABI Bio Bags Udyog emerged from a need for high-micron industrial strength bags that wouldn't leave a footprint. We started with a single extruding line and a clear focus on PBAT integration.
