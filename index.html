Our Material Science

Engineered for Earth, Built for Strength.

At ABI Bio Bags Udyog, we leverage the advanced molecular structure of PBAT (Polybutylene Adipate Terephthalate) blended with natural biofillers to create packaging that rivals traditional plastic in durability while leaving zero ecological footprint.

Our manufacturing process is optimized for precision transparency, ensuring every bag meets rigorous environmental certifications without compromising on load-bearing capacity or texture quality.
